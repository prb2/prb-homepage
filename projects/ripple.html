Description
We've all been in that situation where we need to control our computers, but can't use traditional input methods like a mouse or keyboard. Whether you're in the middle of kneading dough or presenting a report, you can use Ripple to control your web browser. Instead of dirtying your keyboard or mouse, Ripple utilizes a new input method: the Pebble Smartwatch. Through a Google Chrome Extension and a Pebble watch app, Ripple allows you to use gestures and button clicks on your Pebble to scroll webpages and toggle play/pause on YouTube.
Demo
Gestures
Raise Arm: Scroll Webpage Up
Lower Arm: Scroll Webpage Down
Buttons
Double Click Middle Button: Activate Gestures
Long-hold Up Button: Scroll Webpage Up
Long-hold Down Button: Scroll Webpage Down
Double Click Up Button: Next Video (YouTube)
Double Click Down Button: Previous Video (YouTube)
Middle Button: Toggle Play/Pause
